Spaghetti Carbonara

A rich and moist chocolate cake that satisfies your sweet tooth. Great for birthdays or any special occasion.

Ingredients

  1. 200g spaghetti
  2. 100g pancetta
  3. 2 large eggs
  4. 50g Pecorino cheese
  5. 50g Parmesan cheese
  6. Freshly ground black pepper
  7. Salt

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a pot of salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, cook the pancetta in a pan until crisp. Remove from heat.
  3. Whisk the eggs, Pecorino, Parmesan, and black pepper together in a bowl.
  4. Drain the pasta and immediately toss it with the pancetta, then stir in the egg mixture.
  5. Serve hot with additional cheese and pepper on top.

Vegetable Stir Fry

A colorful, healthy, and quick stir fry packed with fresh vegetables. Perfect for a weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  1. 1 red bell pepper
  2. 1 yellow bell pepper
  3. 1 carrot
  4. 200g broccoli florets
  5. 2 tbsp soy sauce
  6. 1 tbsp olive oil
  7. 2 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Chop all the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the vegetables and stir fry for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Drizzle with soy sauce and stir to combine. Serve hot.

Chocolate Cake

A rich and moist chocolate cake that satisfies your sweet tooth. Great for birthdays or any special occasion.

Ingredients

  1. 200g flour
  2. 200g sugar
  3. 100g cocoa powder
  4. 3 eggs
  5. 150g butter
  6. 1 tsp baking powder
  7. 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour a cake tin.
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder in a large bowl.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs, butter, and vanilla extract together.
  4. Slowly fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until well combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the cake tin and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Let the cake cool before serving.
